About Me

Hey everyone! My name is Rithvik, and I am a high school junior with a deep passion for computer science and technology. I am always looking for opportunities to expand my knowledge and develop new skills in the field. Outside of academics, I enjoy playing video games, spending time with friends, and watching movies or TV shows. Through this blog, I look forward to sharing my journey, experiences, and insights as I explore the ever-evolving world of technology.

Music

Music has been an integral part of my life for as long as I can remember. I have experience playing multiple instruments, including the piano, guitar, and trumpet. My musical journey began at the age of five when I started learning the piano, which helped me discover my passion for music. Over the years, I expanded my skills by picking up the guitar and later the trumpet during middle school.

In addition to instrumental music, I have been a dedicated Carnatic vocalist for over a decade. I have performed in numerous concerts and competitions, earning recognition and several awards for my performances. Music continues to be a significant part of my life, and I deeply appreciate its ability to inspire, connect, and express emotions.

Sports

I have always enjoyed staying active and playing sports. Some of my favorite sports include basketball, soccer, tennis, and cricket. I used to compete in tennis tournaments and achieved considerable success in the sport. While I no longer compete at a high level, I still love playing sports recreationally with friends.

Beyond playing, I am an avid sports fan and enjoy keeping up with the latest sports news, matches, and player updates. Whether it's watching a thrilling game or discussing strategies and team performances, sports remain an exciting and important part of my life.
